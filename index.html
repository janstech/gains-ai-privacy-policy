Privacy Policy

This page explains what data GainsAI processes, why it is processed, and how you can contact support.

Introduction

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application GainsAI.

Summary

  • GainsAI uses technical authentication and security systems to protect the app and backend.
  • Training-related data you create in the app may be stored securely in the backend to provide core functionality.
  • AI features use your provided training/profile inputs to generate plans, guidance, and meal-related results.
  • Optional Google Drive backup and optional Health Connect integration are used only when enabled by you.

Data Collection and Usage

GainsAI processes data that is required to deliver its core features, such as training plan generation, workout logging, progress analytics, recommendations, meal planning, and backup/restore.

Depending on how you use the app, this may include profile information, training preferences, workout history, saved programs, substitution preferences, nutrition-related preferences, body metrics, and weekly check-in responses.

Authentication and Technical Identification

The application uses Firebase Authentication, including anonymous authentication, together with technical identifiers needed to secure requests and associate app data with your app instance.

This can include a Firebase user identifier, app-generated device identifier, authentication token, and security verification tokens.

AI Features

GainsAI includes AI-assisted features such as training plan generation, recommendations, and meal plan related functionality. When you use these features, relevant inputs may be transmitted to a secure backend service for processing.

These inputs may include information such as your goal, experience level, training frequency, training preferences, nutrition preferences, meal-plan preferences, and other text or structured inputs you submit in the app.

AI-generated outputs are used to provide the requested in-app functionality and are not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Health and Fitness Data

GainsAI is a health and fitness related application. Depending on your usage, the app may process body metrics, workout performance data, nutrition-related preferences, weekly self-reported training/recovery inputs, and related progress data.

This data is processed to provide core app features such as plan generation, workout tracking, progress analytics, recommendations, body metrics views, and weekly check-in summaries.

Security & Anti-Abuse

To protect the service and prevent abuse, GainsAI may process technical security data such as:

  • Firebase App Check token to verify that requests come from a legitimate app instance.
  • Google Play Integrity token + nonce to help identify tampered or untrusted environments.
  • Authentication tokens and request identifiers to authorize and trace requests securely.
  • Technical identifiers, request counters, and timestamps for service protection and rate limiting.

These checks are used for security, integrity, and abuse prevention purposes only.

Google Drive Backup & Restore

GainsAI may offer optional Google Drive backup and restore functionality. When enabled by you, selected app data may be stored in your Google Drive app data area for backup purposes.

This feature is optional and used only when you choose to sign in and use backup/restore.

Health Connect

GainsAI may support optional Health Connect integration, for example for weight-related data where supported by the device and user permissions.

Health Connect access is optional and only used when you explicitly grant permission.

Third-Party Services

The application may rely on third-party services such as Firebase Authentication, Firebase App Check, Google Play Integrity, Google Drive APIs, optional Health Connect APIs, and AI service providers used through the backend.

Data Retention

Data may be retained as long as necessary to provide app functionality, maintain your training history and related features, protect the service, and support optional backup/restore behavior.

Limited technical logs, rate-limit records, and security-related metadata may also be processed for service protection purposes.

Fitness & AI Disclaimer

GainsAI provides fitness, training, and nutrition-related content for general informational purposes only.

AI-generated outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for your individual situation. You are responsible for using your own judgment and, where appropriate, consulting qualified professionals.

The application does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
